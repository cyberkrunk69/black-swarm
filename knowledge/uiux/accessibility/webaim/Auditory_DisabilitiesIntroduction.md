## Something to Think About...

![](media/ear.jpg)When we think of accessibility, we often envision a blind user using a screen reader. Users who are deaf or hard-of-hearing are easily overlooked. With video and multimedia content so prevalent online, it's important that users with auditory disabilities be considered.

## Solutions

The most important consideration to make content accessible to users with hearing impairment is very straightforward - provide [captions](/techniques/captions/) for multimedia content (meaning video content that also has audio) and provide [transcripts](/techniques/captions/#transcripts) for all audio content. A sign language version of the media may also be provided for an even more inclusive experience (assuming visitors understand the version of sign language provided).
